RUSSIAN DRUNKEN BEARS

Morning comrades!

This project is about Russia, vodka and drip bears

Story

Far future, animals aquired mind and start building their own civilization on ruin of their human predecessors. Sometimes different species fight each other. But our dudes just don't care, they want some vodka and bear-woman to spend time with.

Collection structure

Project consists of 10,000 bears generated by programmed algorithm. There are 3 races: bear, skele-bear and demon bear. Each bear has 6 types of featuress to differ their rarity ( outfit, accessories, glasses, drink, race and boots).

Team

Nothing special just 2 average NFT enjoyers from Russia :)
